About
Van Tech Coding Club was established in October of 2022. Our mission is to make programming accessible and fun to learn for everyone. We can be found in Room 601 every Wednesday, as well as on Instagram @vtcodingclub! Feel free to join us if you want to learn some basics of topics like Python and Web Development, or just have a welcoming place to hang out! No prior experience required.
